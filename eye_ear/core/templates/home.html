FAQs

Who writes on My Audio Blogs?

Anyone can write on My Audio Blogs. Whether you’ve never written before or are ready to create a full publication, it’s easy to get started and we allow you to focus more on big ideas and less on driving clicks.

How do I start writing on My Audio Blogs?

First, make a free account. Then, to create a blog, you can click the "Create Blog" button in the navbar or banner of the home page.

Who can read the work I publish on My Audio Blogs?

Anyone on the internet can read your stories if it's public else only you can read them :)

Can the author add their own voice also instead of the automatically generated transcript?

Yes, authors can add their own voice also instead of the automatically generated transcript by clicking on the Add Audio button on the editing blog page.

What is the pricing of using all services?

0, Zero, nothing, nill... This platform is completely free!!
